My Top 10 Shows to Binge-watch

A collection of the shows I love the most and can watch forever and ever!

1. Sex and the City

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A fun, iconic series about friendship, love, and life in NYC. Carrie: A blogger who lives for shoes and her long-term situationship. Charlotte: Lover girl and art collector. Miranda: Lawyer and a realist. Samantha: Wild and free.

2. Abbott Elementary

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A hilarious mockumentary about teachers at an underfunded public school. The chemistry between Janine and Gregory will keep you on your toe.

3. Schitts Creek

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★

A rich family gone poor. When they realize the town they bought is literally schitty...they grow closer together by trying to sell the town.

4. The Good Place

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★

What happens in the after life? Are you going to be in the "good" or "bad" place? This comedy brings a philosophical scenario with a twist.

5. Brooklyn Nine Nine

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A sit-com about the New York Police Department. Each episode bringing new and unique cases, showcasing the department's different personality.

6. Superstore

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A funky grocery store sit-com. Makes you wonder what goes in your local walmart.

7. All American 4

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A high school star footballer moves to Beverly Hills and learns the rope from the rich kids. Full of drama from high school to college.

8. Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A korean drama, about a women who moves to a small town to build a dentist clinic. She meets a man who does...everything! The cutest love story.

9. Nurse Jackie

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A nurse who is addicted to drugs. The irony. Full of scandals just from one overworked nurse.

10. The Penthouse

Binge Worthiness: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Every episode keeps you on your toe. From one rich family to another. Plus murder mysteries...
