JT's top five albums

Albums I can listen to over and over again.

  1. "Souled Out: 10th Anniversary Edition" by Jhene Aiko This beautifully written album was released originally in 2014, but was remade in 2024 to feature a new song. I'm biased because Jhené Aiko is my favorite artist in general, but this album is one that brings positive energy and tunes! The mixture of the melody and the lyrics will always be my favorite.
  2. "The Seven Deadly Sins by Shreea Kaul Shreea Kaul is an R&B artist combining Western and South Asian music together. This gorgeous album portrays the theme of the seven deadly sins and romance.
  3. "Lemonade" by Beyoncé I had a class in high school that studied the entirety of this album and music videos. It was a great project, an even greater album. From the symbolism and Beyoncé's voice, everything is perfect.
  4. "Florida's Finest" by SAILORR SAILORR is a new, upcoming Vietnamese American artist who is adding her voice to the R&B industry. This album is fun and catchy! One of the songs, POOKIE'S REQUIEM, had gone viral on tiktok as well.
  5. "niteharts" by ISOXO This album is electric! Combining two of my ultimate favorite EDM artists, it is a great album to listen to during a long car ride.
